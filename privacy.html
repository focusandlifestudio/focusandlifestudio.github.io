Privacy Policy

Focus & Life Studio

Last updated: 07 September 2025

We operate tools that help create, schedule, and publish Pins to our own Pinterest account and link those Pins to our Etsy listings. This page explains what we do and do not process when you use our content or visit our pages.

What we process

  • Pin assets we generate for our own Pinterest account (images, titles, descriptions, links).
  • Public listing data from our Etsy shop that we use to build Pin captions and links.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect personal visitor data on this site.
  • We do not track you across sites and we do not sell personal information.

Data sources and destinations

  • We pull product details from our own Etsy shop to construct Pins.
  • We publish Pins only to our own Pinterest business account.

Payments and orders

Purchases and checkout are handled entirely by Etsy. For any order data, payments, or buyer information, Etsy’s own privacy policy and terms apply.

Retention

We keep only the Pin assets and configuration necessary to post to our Pinterest account. We do not store customer or visitor personal data.

Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Pin assets are shared only with Pinterest for publication to our own account.

Children

Our content and tools are intended for general audiences and are not directed to children.
